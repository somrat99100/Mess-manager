What it does

Everything your meal register did — now shared, live, and automatic

Every calculation your Excel sheet used to do by hand happens instantly, for everyone in the mess.

🗓️

Daily meal log

Lunch and dinner toggles for every member, defaulting ON — just switch OFF when someone's away.

💸

Expense tracking

Log every purchase by category — fish, vegetables, spices, groceries — with running totals.

📊

Live dashboard

Total collection, total expense, meal rate, and remaining fund — recalculated the moment anything changes.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑

Member balances

Each member's deposit minus their meal cost, so who owes what is never a guessing game.

🔐

Two roles, one account

Every manager is still a member of their own mess — their meals count too. Members get a simpler, focused view.

📱

Works everywhere

Phone, tablet, or laptop — the whole mess can check balances without asking anyone for the sheet.

How it works

From empty account to running mess in five steps

01

Create your account

Sign up with your name, email, and a password. This is your one login — it can hold both a member profile and, later, a manager profile.

Everyone
02

Create or join a mess

Start a new mess (you become its manager automatically) or enter the invite code a friend shares with you to join theirs as a member.

Everyone
03

Set the meal deadline

As manager, choose the daily cutoff time (e.g. 10 PM) after which meal counts lock for the next day — matching how a real mess plans its shopping.

Manager
04

Members toggle their own meals

Before the deadline, each member switches their own lunch/dinner ON or OFF. After the deadline, only the manager can adjust it.

Member
05

Manager logs expenses, everyone sees balances

The manager records purchases as they happen. The dashboard recalculates meal rate and every member's balance automatically.

Manager

Your mess deserves better than a shared spreadsheet

Free to start. No app to install. Just a link everyone in your mess can open.
